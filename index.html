COMP2132 Hangman - Logan D-A

Game Summary

You have been assigned a partner in your class to guess a word in a hangman game. He is nervous since he needs to guess it correctly to pass the class. You get 6 total guesses to guess the word. Run out of guesses... you guessed it... YOU LOSE! and your partner fails the class! you dont want to do that to him... do you?
